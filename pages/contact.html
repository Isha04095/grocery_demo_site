Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our service

Our average delivery time is 12 minutes from order confirmation. During peak hours, it may take up to 20 minutes. We'll always provide you with an accurate estimate at checkout.

We currently deliver to 50+ neighborhoods across the metropolitan area. Use our service area checker above to see if we deliver to your location and get specific delivery details.

We offer a 100% freshness guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with any item, contact us within 24 hours and we'll provide a full refund or replacement at no extra cost.

After placing your order, you'll receive SMS and email updates with real-time tracking. You can also track your order through our website or mobile app using your order number.

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and digital wallets. All payments are processed securely with SSL encryption.
